# Kitchen Sync — User Guide

A screen-by-screen walkthrough of everything you can do in Kitchen Sync, your household meal planning app.

---

## Getting Started

### Sign-In

Kitchen Sync uses your Google account to sign in. Tap the **Google sign-in** button on the welcome screen and you'll be taken straight into the app.

- On your first launch, you'll land on the **Recipes** tab
- On return visits, you'll land on whichever tab you used last
- A default household is created for you automatically — no setup needed

### Navigating the App

Kitchen Sync has three main tabs along the bottom of the screen and a profile menu in the top-right corner.

- **Recipes** tab — browse, search, create, and edit your recipe collection
- **Meal Plan** tab — plan meals on a day-by-day timeline
- **Shopping List** tab — see all ingredients you need, computed from your meal plan
- **Profile icon** (top-right) — opens a menu with Manage Household, Settings, and Sign Out

---

## Recipes

### Browsing Recipes

The Recipes tab shows all the recipes in your household's collection. An **Add Recipe** card appears as the first item, followed by your saved recipe cards.

- Tap any recipe card to open it and see the full details
- Tap **Add Recipe** to create a new recipe
- If you haven't added any recipes yet, placeholder cards show what the list will look like once you do

### Viewing a Recipe

The Recipe Detail screen shows the full recipe in a clean, readable layout with sections for Title, Intro, Ingredients, Preparation, and Cooking.

- Read through the recipe's sections, formatted for easy following
- Tap **Edit** to make changes to the recipe
- Any household member can view and edit any recipe

### Creating & Editing Recipes

The Recipe Editor lets you type or paste recipe content into labeled section fields. Gray placeholder text guides you on what goes in each section.

- Fill in any combination of **Title**, **Intro**, **Ingredients**, **Preparation**, and **Cooking**
- Use the formatting toolbar to add **bold** text or bullet lists
- Tap **Save** to save your recipe, or **Cancel** to discard changes
- When editing an existing recipe, your changes create a new revision — nothing is lost

### Smart Paste & AI Formatting

If you have a recipe from a website or message, you can paste it directly into the editor and let AI restructure it into the right sections.

- Paste unstructured recipe text into the Title field of an empty editor
- The app splits the first line into the Title and the rest into Intro automatically
- Tap the **AI Restructure** button to have AI reformat the text into proper sections
- Review the AI's suggested formatting on a full-screen preview — tap **Approve** to accept or **Cancel** to keep your original text

### Searching Recipes

Use the search bar at the top of the Recipes tab to find recipes quickly.

- Tap the search bar and type your query — the Add Recipe card hides while you search
- Search matches across all recipe fields (title, ingredients, preparation, etc.)
- Results show the recipe name plus relevant context from matching sections
- Tap a result to open the full recipe

## Meal Planning

### The Timeline

The Meal Plan tab shows an infinitely scrolling timeline of days, anchored to today. Future days are below, past days are above. Each day shows a day-of-week label, date, and any meals you've planned.

- Scroll up to see past meals or down to see upcoming ones
- Tap the **+** button on any day to add a meal
- Tap any meal to expand it and see more options

### Adding a Meal

When you tap **+** on a day, a bottom sheet slides up with your recipe collection so you can pick what to cook.

- Browse your recipes or use the search bar to find one
- A random recipe suggestion is shown if you need inspiration
- Tap a recipe to add it to that day — its ingredients will automatically appear on your shopping list
- Swipe down or tap outside the sheet to dismiss without adding anything

### Managing a Meal

Tap a meal on the timeline to expand it inline. Only one meal can be expanded at a time.

- Tap **View** to open the full recipe in Recipe Detail
- Tap **Edit** to reveal additional actions:
  - **Date** — reschedule this meal to a different day
  - **Delete** — remove this meal from the plan entirely

### Rescheduling a Meal

After tapping **Date** on an expanded meal, a date picker lets you move the meal to a different day.

- Select a new date from the calendar
- Tap **Confirm** to move the meal, or **Cancel** to keep it where it is
- The meal moves to the new day on the timeline, and the shopping list updates automatically

### Deleting a Meal

When you delete a meal, a placeholder card appears in its slot giving you a chance to undo.

- Tap **Undo** to restore the meal
- Tap **X** to dismiss the placeholder and confirm the deletion
- Once dismissed, the meal is permanently removed

## Shopping List

### Using the Shopping List

The Shopping List tab shows all ingredients from your meal plan entries, computed fresh each time you open it. There's nothing to set up — just plan meals and your list appears.

- Check off items as you shop — checked items get a gray strikethrough and sink below unchecked items
- Tap an item's name to copy it to your clipboard (handy for pasting into store apps)
- Tap the meal date badge on any item to see which recipe and meal plan day it came from, then tap to jump to that day in the Meal Plan
- Tap **+** at the bottom to add a manual item that isn't from a recipe
- Deleting a meal from the Meal Plan removes its ingredients from this list

## Household & Settings

### Profile Menu

Tap the profile icon in the top-right corner of any screen to open the profile menu.

- **Manage Household** — view and manage household members
- **Settings** — adjust app settings
- **Sign Out** — sign out of your account and return to the sign-in screen

### Managing Your Household

The Manage Household screen shows everyone in your household. If you're the owner, you can also generate invite links from here.

- See a list of all household members
- Tap any member to view their details
- If you're the owner, tap **Generate Link** to create an invite link (see Inviting Someone below)

### Member Details

The Member Detail screen shows information about a household member. What you see depends on your role.

- **If you're the owner**: view the member's information
- **If you're a member**: you'll see a **Leave Household** option on your own detail screen — tap it and confirm to leave (your data stays in the household)

### Inviting Someone

Only the household owner can invite new members. Inviting is done through a shareable link.

- Go to **Manage Household** and tap **Generate Link**
- The invite link appears inline — tap it to copy
- Share the link with someone via text, email, or any messaging app
- When they open the link, they'll be prompted to sign in (if needed) and will automatically join your household

### Settings

The Settings screen lets you customize your app experience.

- Toggle **Dark mode** between on, off, or system default
